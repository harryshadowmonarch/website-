Twinkling Watermelon 2024

Genre: Time-Travel Fantasy Romance

Starring: Ryeoun, Choi Hyun-wook, Seol In-ah, Shin Eun-soo

Synopsis: Ha Eun-gyeol, a talented musician, unexpectedly time-travels back to 1995. There, he encounters his younger father and forms a band called Watermelon Sugar with a group of high school students. As he navigates the past, he witnesses his parents' first love and strives to understand their choices, hoping to influence their future for the better. Through music, friendship, and a touch of time-bending magic, Eun-gyeol embarks on a heartwarming journey of self-discovery and family bonds.
