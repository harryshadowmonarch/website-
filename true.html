True Beauty 2020-2021

Genre: Romance, Comedy, School, Youth

Starring: Moon Ga-young, Cha Eun-woo, Hwang In-yeop

Synopsis: This romantic comedy-drama follows the life of Lim Ju-kyung, a high school girl who becomes a makeup expert to transform herself into a beauty after being bullied for her looks. As she navigates her new life, she catches the attention of two popular boys, leading to a love triangle. The series explores themes of self-acceptance, beauty standards, and the challenges of adolescence.
