Vincenzo 2021

Genre: Crime, Dark Comedy, Action

Starring: Song Joong-ki, Jeon Yeo-been, Ok Taec-yeon

Synopsis: The story centers on Vincenzo Cassano, a Korean-Italian mafia lawyer who returns to South Korea after a betrayal in Italy. He seeks to reclaim a hidden treasure buried in a building controlled by a corrupt conglomerate. Teaming up with a group of quirky tenants, Vincenzo uses his mafia skills to take down the villains and deliver justice in his own unique way. The series blends dark comedy, action, and romance.
