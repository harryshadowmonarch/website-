I Am Not a Robot 2017-2018

Genre: Romance, Comedy, Sci-Fi

Starring: Yoo Seung-ho, Chae Soo-bin, Uhm Ki-joon

Synopsis:This romantic comedy revolves around a wealthy CEO, Kim Min-kyu, who has a severe allergy to human contact. To help him, a woman named Jo Ji-ah pretends to be a robot. As they navigate their unique relationship, Ji-ah must maintain her disguise while developing feelings for Min-kyu. The series explores themes of love, trust, and the challenges of human connection..
