Death's Game 2023

Genre: Thriller, Mystery

Starring: Seo In-guk, Park So-dam

Synopsis: Death's Game follows Choi Yi-jae, a man who, after seven years of job hunting failures and personal despair, contemplates suicide. However, he is confronted by Death, who gives him a unique punishment: he must experience death in twelve different lives. Through these reincarnations , Yi-jae inhabits the bodies of various individuals facing imminent death, each with their own stories and challenges.
